Joseph Snyder is a versatile programmer with a unique background in both media arts and computer science. He embarked on his academic journey by earning a Bachelor's Degree in Film, Video and New Media from the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee. During this time, Joseph honed his skills in digital media production, gaining proficiency in video editing, multimedia design, and new media technologies. Building on his passion for media arts, Joseph pursued a Master's Degree in Film, Video and New Media at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Here, he delved deeper into experimental media, interactive installations, and advanced digital storytelling techniques. His academic journey in media arts provided him with a strong foundation in creativity, visual aesthetics, and the technical aspects of multimedia production. Eager to expand his expertise into the realm of technology, Joseph Snyder then pursued a second Master's Degree, this time in Computer Science, at the Illinois Institute of Technology. This marked a significant shift in his career focus, as he immersed himself in the world of software development, algorithms, and computer systems. During his studies, he specialized in areas such as web development, C++, JavaScript, and broader IT concepts, consolidating his technical skills and knowledge. Joseph's dual background in media arts and computer science uniquely positions him at the intersection of creativity and technology. His proficiency in programming languages and IT fundamentals complements his creative instincts and understanding of digital media, allowing him to approach projects with a well-rounded perspective. Whether developing interactive media applications, implementing complex algorithms, or crafting compelling visual narratives, Joseph Snyder brings a blend of artistic vision and technical expertise to his work. Outside of academia, Joseph is known for his passion for exploring innovative ways to merge media arts with cutting-edge technologies, aiming to push the boundaries of digital experiences and storytelling. His journey from a film and media enthusiast to a skilled programmer reflects his commitment to continuous learning and interdisciplinary exploration in the rapidly evolving fields of media and technology.
